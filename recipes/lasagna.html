Lasagna

How to Make Lasagna

Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait. You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below, so let's go!

Ingredients

  • Meat: 1 3/4 pound of ground beef
  • Onion and Garlic: 1/2 cup minced onion, 2 cloves crushed garlic
  • Tomato products: 1(28oz) can crushed tomatoes, 2(6.5oz)cans tomato sauce, 2 (6oz) cans tomato paste
  • Sugar: 2 tablespoons
  • Spices and Seasonings: 4 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley, 1 1/2 teaspoons dried basil leaves, 1 1/2 teaspoons salt, 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning, 1/2 teaspoon fennel seeds, 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • Lasagna noodles: 12 lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses: 16oz ricotta cheese, 3/4 pound mozzarella cheese sliced, 3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Egg: 1 egg

Directions

Step 1

Gather all your ingredients.

Step 2

Making the sauce. Cook meet, onion, and garlic over medium heat until well browned.

Step 3

In same pot, stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, and 1/2 cup water. Season with sugar, 2 tablespoons parsley, basil, 1 teaspoon salt, Italian seasoning, fennel, seeds, and pepper. Simmer, covered, for about 1 1/2 hours, stirring occasionally.

Step 4

Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook lasagna noodles in boiling water for 8 to 10 minutes. Drain noodles, and rinse with cold water.

Step 5

In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese with egg, remaining 2 tablespoons parsley, and 1/2 teaspoon salt.

Step 6

Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F° (190 degrees C°)

Step 7

To assemble, spread 1 1/2 cups pf meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish Arrange 6 noodles lengthwise over meat sauce. Spread with 1/2 of the ricotta cheese mixture. Top with 1/3 of the mozzarella cheese slices. Spoon 1 1/2 cups meat sauce over mozzarella, and sprinkle with 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese.

Step 8

Repeat Layers, and top with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese. cover with foil: to prevent sticking, either spray foil with cooking spray or make sure the foil does not touch the cheese. *You don't want to miss out on the cheese goodness!*

Step 9

Bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes. Or until the top is golden brown.

Step 10

Rest lasagna for 15 minutes before serving.
